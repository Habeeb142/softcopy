Know Us Better

Softcopy.ng is a researchers’ aid, e-publishing house and e-book retailer known for its tested integrity, excellence and independence. By and large, Softcopy.ng is divided into three arms: Soft Researchers, Soft Readers and Smart Researchers.

Soft Researchers is the arm that solely centres on assisting researchers; Soft Readers mainly focuses on enhancing literacy and info-education; while Smart Researchers is devoted to developing young researchers to have interest and advance in making research.

Softcopy.ng is the first Nigerian researchers’ aid, e-publishing house and e-book store that penetrates various nations of the world. Being an e-publishing house, we offer services for publishing manuscripts into several e-book formats and market them online as well. You can read our e-books online and offline in our Soft Reader or download them to any device of your choice which accepts e-book formats like EPUB, MOBI/PRC (Mobi Pocket) and PDF (Adobe’s Portable Document Format). You can as well play or download our audio books (Mp3, M4a, Mp4 and Avi).
